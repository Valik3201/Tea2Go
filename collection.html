Introducing Our New Summer Blend: Citrus Sunset

Welcome the warmth of summer with open arms and tantalize your taste buds with our latest creation - Citrus Sunset. Overflowing with refreshing and fruity goodness, this limited-time tea is designed to bring a burst of sunshine to your daily tea ritual.

Limited-Time Delight - Act Now!

Don't let this seasonal treasure slip away! Our New Citrus Sunset is available for a limited time only. Embrace the opportunity to immerse yourself in the delightful flavors of summer. Seize the moment and savor the joy of this exclusive blend.

Taste the Summer Today!

Savor our Citrus Sunset blend - a vibrant infusion of ripe grapefruit and rich black tea.

  • The Energetic Zing of Grapefruit:

    Sun-kissed and bursting with vitality, the citrusy notes of fresh grapefruit dance on your palate, invigorating your senses with every sip. The juicy sweetness and tangy brightness of grapefruit awaken your taste buds and leave a refreshing trail of zest.

  • Elevating with Premium Black Tea:

    To balance and enhance the grapefruit's liveliness, we artfully blend it with premium black tea leaves. Sourced from select tea gardens, these leaves undergo expert processing, resulting in a full-bodied and robust tea base that provides a satisfying depth to the blend.

  • A Symphony of Summer Flavors:

    The combination of grapefruit and black tea creates a harmonious symphony of flavors, where the vivacity of citrus fuses elegantly with the comforting richness of tea. This delightful union captures the essence of a beautiful summer sunset, leaving a lingering sense of warmth and contentment.

  • Savor the Moment:

    With every cup of Citrus Sunset, immerse yourself in the vivid hues and enchanting flavors of summer. Whether enjoyed hot or iced, this blend promises an invigorating and uplifting experience that celebrates the vibrant energy of the season.
